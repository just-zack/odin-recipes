Lentil Burgers

A healthy alternative to a beef burger

Lentil burgers are healthier for your body and the planet! They are full of umami flavor while being packed with protein and fiber. After eating one of these Lentil Burgers you won't go back to beef!

Lentils are packed full of healthy amino acids and are easily digestible. It doesn't hurt too that the environmental impact of a Lentil is far smaller than raising cows.

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Sunflower Seeds
  • Walnuts
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Flour
  • Oregano
  • Thyme
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Using a food processor, finely chop carrot, onion, garlic, walnuts, and sunflower seeds.
  2. In a large bowl, combine these ingredients with cooked lentils, breadcrumbs, herbs, and salt.
  3. Add egg, tomato paste, and Soy sauce, then stir in flour.
  4. Allow the mixture to rest for 30 minutes before forming into patties.
  5. Cook patties in 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat for 8-10 minutes, flipping once.
  6. Plate and serve with your favorite burger garnishes and condiments
