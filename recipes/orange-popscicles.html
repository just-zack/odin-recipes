Orange Popscicle

A refreshing and tart summer treat

Oh these juicy orange popsicles are so delicious and will keep you cool and refreshed! These orange pops are totally the way to go because theyâ€™re super delicious and made from fresh orange juice. Fresh squeezed orange juice is the best because of the bright, fresh flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 2 1/2 cups fresh orange juice
  • 1/8 tsp kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Combine sugar and waterin a bowl
  2. Add orange juice, salt and mix
  3. Pour mix into popscicle molds
  4. Add popscicle sticks
  5. Freeze
  6. Enjoy!
