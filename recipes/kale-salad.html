Kale Salad

A filling and nutritious salad

Packed with dark leafy greens, healthy fats, and fresh lemon juice, it hits the spot every time. Trust me when I say, this salad will turn any kale-hater into a kale lover!

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Avocados
  • Lemon Juice
  • Pine Nuts
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Mash the avocado in a bowl
  2. Add lemon juice, garlic and mix
  3. Add chopped kale, toss lightly
  4. Sprinkle pine nuts
  5. Enjoy!
